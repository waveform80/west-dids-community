What we do

The West Didsbury Estate Community Group is here to:

  • Help to bring the community together
  • Advocate on behalf of all residents about any issues they are having
  • Apply for funding to carry out community projects

Ultimately, we are here to work for you in any capcity we can. We are not miracle workers, but if you are having problems with something, let us know and we'll do what we can to help you.
